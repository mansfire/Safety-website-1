Fill out the form below every day with all inicdents for the time period. COunt the number of fire burn incidents, trip incidents, spill incidents, and cut indicents. An incident may be more than one of these, count it in each applicable category

Fire/burn incident

A burn incident occurs any time a heating/heated element is left on and unattended, a flammable chemical is left out and unattended, or a person/persons recieve a burn.

Trip incident

A Trip incident occurs when a person trips over exposed cables, misplaced equipment, or otherwise mismanaged spaces

Spill incident

A spill incident occurs anytime a chemical (other than water) is spilled from its contianer and requires cleanup

Cut incident

A cut incident occurs when a sharp object or piece of equipment cuts, punctures, pierces, or otherwise damages a person's clothing, skin, or body. Bleeding may or may not occur
